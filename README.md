# 🏪 Super Store Analysis Dashboard (Power BI)

## 📌 Overview

The **Super Store Analysis Dashboard** is an interactive Power BI report designed to provide business stakeholders with a comprehensive overview of sales performance, profitability, customer segments, and shipping trends.

The dashboard enables users to quickly identify top-performing locations, products, and customer segments while monitoring key business KPIs through interactive filters and visualizations.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Monitor overall business performance
- Track revenue, profit and profit margin
- Identify top-performing states and cities
- Analyse product category performance
- Understand customer purchasing segments
- Evaluate shipping method distribution

---

## 🛠️ Tools Used

- **Power BI Desktop**
- Power Query
- DAX
- Excel/CSV (Data Source)

---

## 📊 Dashboard Features

### Executive KPIs
Provides a high-level overview of business performance including:

- Total Revenue
- Total Quantity Sold
- Total Profit
- Profit Margin

---

### Sales by State

Interactive map showing sales performance across all US states.

**Business Value**

- Identify highest revenue states
- Compare regional performance
- Discover geographic sales opportunities

---

### Sales by City

Bar chart displaying the highest revenue generating cities.

**Top Performing Cities include:**

- New York City
- Los Angeles
- Seattle
- San Francisco
- Philadelphia

---

### Sales by Sub-Category

Ranks product sub-categories by total sales.

Top selling categories include:

- Phones
- Chairs
- Storage
- Tables
- Binders

This helps identify products driving revenue and inventory priorities.

---

### Sales by Ship Mode

Breakdown of orders by shipping method.

Includes:

- Standard Class
- Second Class
- First Class
- Same Day

Useful for analysing customer delivery preferences.

---

### Sales by Customer Segment

Displays sales distribution across customer groups:

- Consumer
- Corporate
- Home Office

Helps understand which customer segments contribute most to overall revenue.

---

## 🎛️ Interactive Features

Users can filter the dashboard using:

- Product Category
    - Furniture
    - Office Supplies
    - Technology

All visuals update dynamically based on the selected category.

---

## 📌 Business Insights

Some key insights generated from this dashboard include:

- Consumer segment generates the highest share of sales.
- Standard Class is the most frequently used shipping method.
- California is the highest revenue generating state.
- New York City is the top performing city.
- Phones and Chairs are the highest selling product sub-categories.
- The business maintains an overall profit margin of **11.09%**.

2. Open **Super Store Analysis.pbix** using Power BI Desktop.
3. If prompted, reconnect the Excel dataset located in the **data** folder.
4. Refresh the report to load the latest data.
